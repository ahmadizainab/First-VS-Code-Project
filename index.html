The Cutest Cats

Scottish Fold

The Scottish Fold originates in Scotland. Its unique feature is caused by a genetic mutation which affects the cartilage in its body, creating its adorable ear folds.

Ragdoll

Ragdolls are for famous their silky, soft and semi-long hair. They were created by Ann Baker during the 1960s, in the United States. They are known to be very peaceful and sweet cats.

Munchkin

Munchkin cats are popular for their short and stubby legs, which are due to a genetic mutation. They may be small but they can do anything that other cats can do!
